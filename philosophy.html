Problem Statement

It‚Äôs a common sight on any college campus. Someone loses a jacket, an ID card, or a charger, and the search turns into a chain of WhatsApp messages, asking friends, or scanning notice boards. This process is slow, messy, and often leads nowhere. We noticed that at our own college, items would remain unclaimed for days or even weeks simply because there was no central place for lost and found information. The lack of a structured system meant even if someone had found the item, the chances of it being returned to the right person were slim.

Core Objectives:

  • Eliminate the frustration of losing valuable items
  • Create a trustworthy verification system
  • Build community through helping others
  • Provide fair redistribution through auctions

Our Solution

To bridge this gap, we decided to build a dedicated Lost & Found website, a single platform where students, faculty, and staff can instantly report a lost or found item. By centralizing the process, we make it easy to browse items, search by category, and connect the finder with the rightful owner. We also added features like admin approval for posts, so only genuine and verified listings appear, making the platform not just fast and accessible, but also reliable and secure.

Future Goals:

  • Expand to universities nationwide
  • Integrate AI-powered item matching
  • Create a global lost & found network
  • Establish industry standards for digital L&F

Features That Enhance Usability and Functionality

Colour Blind Mode

We know that traditional color schemes can make it difficult for colorblind users to distinguish important elements. Our colorblind mode adjusts the site‚Äôs palette to high-contrast, universally distinguishable colors, ensuring that buttons, alerts, and categories remain clear for all users.

Large Text Size Mode

Accessibility isn‚Äôt just about colors; it‚Äôs also about readability. Large text size mode gives users the option to enlarge all on-screen text without breaking the design, making the site easier to use for people with visual impairments or those browsing on smaller screens.

Dark Mode

Many people browse in low-light environments or simply prefer darker themes. Dark mode reduces glare, minimizes eye strain during extended use, and makes the site comfortable to navigate at any time of day.

Keystone-Powered Backend

To ensure that all these features work seamlessly and securely, we‚Äôve built our backend using Keystone. It allows us to manage data, user accounts, admin approvals, and listings in a structured way. Keystone gives us both flexibility and control, so the platform remains reliable as we grow and add more institutions in the future.

What Sets Us Apart

Smart Matching

Our system matches lost and found items based on details like category, location, and date, so the right owner is found faster without endless scrolling.

Fair Auctions

For items that remain unclaimed for a long time, we run a transparent auction process so they can still be useful within the community instead of sitting forgotten.

Verified Claims

We have a step-by-step verification process to make sure items go back to their rightful owners and to prevent false claims.

Mobile-First

Since most students use their phones for everything, our site is designed to work smoothly on mobile devices without losing speed or features.
